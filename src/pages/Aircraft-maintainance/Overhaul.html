Overhaul Workshops

We test, repair, modify and overhaul a range of aircraft components in our state-of-the-art workshops. This includes avionics, airframe, cabin furnishings, structures, non-destructive testing (NDT), battery and oxygen charging.

Cabin Workshops

The Cabin Workshops look after the appearance and function of all the interior soft furnishings, seats and safety equipment on the Emirates fleet.

There are several workshops for all aspects of the aircraft:

  • Evacuation and Safety Workshop

    • This is where we maintain all the emergency equipment. We test, repair, overhaul and modify escape slides, rafts, ramps and over-wing slides, life jackets, baby cots, fire extinguishers, oxygen masks and cockpit seats of the A380 & B777 fleet.

    • Nitrogen Cylinder HST & Valve Overhaul Workshop

      • This is where we carry out the required day to day activities of slide raft activation valve overhaul, cylinder hydrostatic requalification and charging of pressure cylinders for emergency evacuation system of the A380 & B777 fleet.

      • Line & Base Operations Support Group

        • Performs cabin & Cargo tasks requiring extensive component repair of A319, A380 & B777 fleet.

        • Heavy Maintenance Support Group

          • Performs cabin components overhaul tasks during '3C' checks of the fleet. Extensive component repair for any C check of A319, A380 and B777 Fleet. This would include disassembly of the major monuments like toilets and bar units to recondition them to new and give them a new lease of life.

          • Cabin General Workshops

            • This where we overhaul passenger seats, galleys, carpets, cabin component composite repair, leather & upholstery repair, cargo components, side wall, general furnishings, galley equipments, and meal carts on the fleet are repaired and reconditioned.

            • Filters, Water & Waste Tube Cleaning Workshop

              • This where we perform cleaning & testing of environmental filters, Water & waste tubes, E/E Cooling filters, waste tank & toilet bowl on the A380, B777 fleet.

Avionics Workshops

Avionics Workshops maintain all the electrical systems on an aircraft. We test, repair, overhaul and modify, A319, A380 and B777 components.

We can test a wide range of components quickly and accurately using state-of-the-art ATEC series 6 and ATEC 5000 automated test stations.

These are the areas of the aircraft our technicians work on in the Avionics Workshop:

  • Computers for flight control and navigation systems
  • CEngine electronic control units
  • Flight data recorder readout and analysis system
  • Software development, EFB and other data loading systems
  • ACE workstations for AIRSHOW 420 and 4200D software development
  • Media-loading stations for Panasonic inflight entertainment systems
  • Galley equipment - ovens, wine chillers, fridges, coffee makers, trash compactors, lights, Harnesses and any other electrical equipment that would need repairs.
  • Passenger entertainment components, noise cancellation and other passenger headsets
  • Radio communication and navigation system components
  • Batteries of various models.

  • Auxiliary power units

This team is responsible for providing technical support services to power plant and APU systems and accessories, including engine and APU condition monitoring.

This involves engine health monitoring, and developing programmes to make sure the engines are overhauled to the highest standards as efficiently as we can, and are back on the wing within deadlines to minimise costs.

The Engine Shops

The Engine Shops perform module replacement, QEC/DE-QEC, modification and minor repair of all engine types used on the Emirates fleet.

They also perform QEC/DE-QEC of all APU types installed on Emirates aircraft and light maintenance of all thrust reversers installed across the fleet.
