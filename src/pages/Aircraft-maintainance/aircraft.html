Aircraft Maintenance

Full support for emirates fleet, and other airlines, from state-of-the-art facility in Dubai and at outstations across the world

Line Maintenance

Technical support for aircraft and engines, and aircraft cabin maintenance and refurbishment. We work 24 hours, seven days a week at our state-of-the-art facility in Dubai and at our network of outstations

Base Maintenance

Carries out all routine, scheduled maintenance tasks and any unscheduled major repairs, replacements and modifications to the Emirates fleet.

Overhaul Workshops

Tests, repairs, modifies and overhauls a range of components in state-of-the-art workshops staffed by highly skilled personnel.

Engine Maintenance Centre

At our power plant, we service more than 300 Emirates engines to keep the fleet in the best possible condition.

Engine Test Cell

A leading edge engine test facility - one of only around twelve such facilities in the world, and the largest test facility of its kind in the Middle East.

Aircraft Appearance Centre

We keep our aircraft looking great inside and out. The Aircraft Appearance Centre is divided into a paint hangar, paint workshop, and a graphics and decal shop.
