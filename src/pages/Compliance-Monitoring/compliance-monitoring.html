Compliance Monitoring

Compliance Monitoring Department

The Compliance Monitoring Engineering (CME) Department monitors all of the
Emirates continuing airworthiness management functions, aircraft maintenance and
engineering support activities to ensure they remain in compliance with engineering
procedures, customer requirements, industry standards, as well as the various
National/International Civil Aviation regulations.

Compliance Monitoring Engineering

Compliance Monitoring – Engineering (CM-E) section is responsible for the overall
compliance oversight of Emirates Engineering through its established Audit Program,
providing an unbiased and autonomous assessment of organisation’s technical
activities in compliance with applicable standards.

Compliance Monitoring – Regulatory Affairs

The Compliance Monitoring Engineering – Regulatory Affairs (CME-RA) section is
responsible for the acquisition, timely renewal, amendment and/or extension of all
organisation approvals necessary for Emirates Engineering to deliver its premium
aircraft engineering products and associated technical services to Emirates fleet and
its global third party operators and customers.

Compliance Monitoring – Certification

The Compliance Monitoring Engineering - Certification (CME-C) section is responsible
for the establishment of Emirates Engineering standards for qualifying and evaluating
certifying staff and issuance of privileges and limitations based on operator’s national
aviation regulations and the level of trainings and extent of experience of individual
certifying staff.
